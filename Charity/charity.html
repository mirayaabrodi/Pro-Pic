Our Charity Partner

The corona pandemic presents us all with enormous challenges. Millions of
people have been infected and in a short time some of the world's most
advanced healthcare systems have been paralyzed.

MSF follows developments and adapts our efforts to current local needs. We
use close to 50 years of experience in saving lives and protecting the most
vulnerable in major disease outbreaks.

That's why we work with the World Health Organization (WHO), so we can
be part of saving the world and doing good deeds.

Our Partner - COVID-19 Solidarity Response Fund for WHO

The world has never faced a crisis like COVID-19. The pandemic affects
communities around the world. Supporting a global response led by the
World Health Organization (WHO) has never been more urgent.

Donations support the work of WHO, including with partners, to track
and understand the spread of the virus; providing patients with the care
they need and receiving the necessary materials and information for
frontline employees; and accelerating the research and development
of vaccines and therapies for all who need them.

Some donations also support additional UNICEF partners, the World
Food Program, the United Nations Refugee Agency (UNHCR), the United
Nations Relief and Works Agency for Palestinian Refugees in the Middle
East (UNRWA) and the CEPI Vaccine Development Alliance.
